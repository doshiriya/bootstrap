An About Us page can include a wide array of information, but the most common sections to include are: Who you serve and what you do for them. Your brand story/history, including how you got started. Your mission and vision for the company.The best About Us pages share the company and founders' stories. It's a chance to pull back the curtain on the business and showcase the people who make it happen. Some of the most effective About Us pages: Connect the consumer to the business on a deeper level.
