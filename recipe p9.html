Q. What are common FAQs?

A. Common FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions) can vary depending on the product, service, or industry.

Q. What is General FAQ?

A. It is a collection of common questions and answers not specific to a product, feature, or service. They usually cover payment policies, how to contact customer support, and refund policies.

Q. What is the frequently asked questions feature?

A. An FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions) page is a key part of a knowledge base because it addresses the most common questions customers have and is useful to customers at all stages of the customer journey. FAQs start with a question and then answer it concisely.
